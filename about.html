Hello, world. I'm Emily

I was a hairstylist for 5+ years and now I’m on my way to become a full stack developer at Ada Developers Academy in Seattle. So far, this has been the best decision of my life.

I love learning something new every day. The challenge of gaining a new skill is an empowering experience of growth. For me, it’s not just about pursuing a new career, but changing the face and increasing diversity in tech.

I love mixing creativity with logic, devoted to finishing a project, and curious about how I can improve the experience for others. When I’m not designing or coding, I love drinking coffee (this is always), get lost in a good book, and go out on family walks with my husband and Golden Retriever, Hugo.
